This program helps health care providers determine dosage of medication based on patient's weight.
Patient came in not feeling well, based on her weight of 115.0 lbs (52.21 kg), the patient was prescribed 1566.3 mg of medication.
Patient number two was also not feeling well, based on her weight of 134.0 lbs (60.836 kg), the patient was prescribed 1825.08 mg of medication.
Patient number three came in with symptoms similar to that of patient two. Based on her weight of 152.0 lbs (69.008 kg), the patient was prescribed 2070.24 mg of medication.
